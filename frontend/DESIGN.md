# Design System: Sacred Elegance (CCIQ Inspired)

This document defines the visual identity and design tokens for the Quran School SaaS, drawing inspiration from modern Islamic architecture and the CCIQ brand.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
*   **Sacred Emerald** (`#064E3B`): Deep, spiritual green used for branding, primary buttons, and heavy backgrounds.
*   **Medina Gold** (`#D4AF37`): Elegant accent color used for highlights, borders, and premium elements.

### Backgrounds
*   **Soft Pearl** (`#FDFCFB`): Warm, minimal light background.
*   **Deep Moss** (`#10221D`): Dark mode background with a subtle green tint.

## Typography

*   **Display / Titles**: `Cinzel` (Serif). Used for headers and brand statements to evoke a sense of tradition and timelessness.
*   **Body / UI**: `Inter` (Sans-serif). Used for readability in forms, dashboards, and general content.

## Design Components

### Corner Radii
*   **Standard**: `1rem` (16px) - For buttons and inputs.
*   **Large**: `2rem` (32px) - For cards and containers.
*   **Extra Large**: `3rem` (48px) - For hero sections.

### Visual Motifs
*   **Islamic Patterns**: Subtle radial geometric patterns used as background overlays.
*   **Soft UI Shadows**: Deep, diffused shadows combined with glassmorphism (backrop-blur).
*   **Gold Linework**: Thin `accent` borders (`1px`) to separate premium sections.
